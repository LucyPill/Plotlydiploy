From Bacteria to Beef

Many bacterial especies are not well known, and many remain unknown to science. Our task is to discover and document these bacteria. We are interested in bacteria especies that have the ability to synthezise proteins that taste like beef. We have have partnered with "Improbable Beef", a food startup to research candidate especies.

Many labs have been succesful in synthesizing meat from algae, fungi, and microorganisms found in plantroots; However, Improbable Beef is still searching for the elusive bacteria that will provide the perfect taste. Our belief is that there is a microorganism that can supply the best next taste and it can be found in bacteria that live in the human body.
